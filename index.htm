About Us

Pedal Forward – Better Biking promotes cycling as a meaningful solution to environmental issues, climate change, and personal health. According to recent studies, increased bicycle use in cities could reduce carbon emissions by millions of tons each year, while improving air quality and reducing traffic. Cycling also contributes to a healthier lifestyle and has been shown to lower the risk of heart disease, obesity, and mental stress.
